# Word List 11

1. flexible adj.灵活的

     - employment n.就业
   adj.柔韧的；可弯曲的；有弹性的

     - copper n.铜

3. focus v.集中(注意力、精力)

     - vocabulary n.词汇
     - grammar n.语法
   n.中心点；焦点；中心
   - The focus of higher education is its equality and teaching reform.
     - equality n.平等
     - reform n.改革

4. fond adj.喜欢的；喜爱的
