## 有限个无穷小的乘积是无穷小

# 极限的四则运算

条件:$\exists\lim f(x)=A, \lim g(x)=B$

- $\lim[f(x)\pm g(x)]=\lim f(x)\pm\lim g(x)=A\pm B$
- $\lim[f(x)\cdot g(x)]=\lim f(x)\cdot\lim g(x)=A\cdot B$
- $\lim[\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}]=\frac{\lim f(x)}{\lim g(x)}=\frac{A}{B}(B\neq0)$
